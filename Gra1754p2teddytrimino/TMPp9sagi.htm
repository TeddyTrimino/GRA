DIY Heart Button Art

Create adorable handmade art using buttons, beads, and a foam heart.

Supplies Needed

  • Picture frame
  • Patterned paper
  • Foam core board
  • Buttons
  • Seed beads
  • Acrylic paint
  • Craft glue or rubber cement

Step-by-Step Instructions

Print or draw a heart pattern. Cut it out and trace it onto foam core board.

Paint the foam heart with acrylic paint in your chosen color.

Apply glue and cover the heart with buttons. Fill gaps with seed beads.

Cut patterned paper to fit your frame and glue the heart onto it.

Let everything dry completely.

Assemble everything and prepare your final framed artwork.
